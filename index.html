Key Rules

🥩

Spread 155g+ across 5–6 meals. Muscles can only use ~40g per sitting for repair.

9–10K steps every day
This is non-negotiable. Daily steps burn an extra 300–400 kcal and improve insulin sensitivity — crucial for fat loss.

Progressive Overload

10-week strength cycle. 2 sets per exercise to start — quality over quantity.

Week 1–2
2 × 10–12
Light — 60% max. Learn the movements. RIR 4+. Steps habit.
Week 3–4
2 × 8–10
Add 5% weight. RIR 2–3. Form should feel solid now.
Week 5–6
3 × 8–10
Add a third set. Add 5% weight. RIR 2. Lateral raises still AMRAP.
Week 7–8
3 × 6–8
Heavier loads — add 5–10%. Push intensity. RIR 1–2.
Week 9
2 × 12 light
⚡ Deload — 50% max. Full recovery. Keep walking steps.
Week 10+
Repeat
New baseline is +10% from start. Repeat from Week 3 with higher weights.

The 4 Rules

RIR
Train by RIR (Reps In Reserve)
RIR 2 = could do 2 more reps with good form. This is your standard intensity. Never go to 0 RIR on compound lifts in the first 4 weeks.
+2
The 2-rep rule
Can complete 2+ extra reps with good form? Add weight next session. Upper +2.5 kg, lower +5 kg.
📝
Track everything
Log weight, sets, reps, and steps every session. You cannot progress what you don't measure.
🚫
Never sacrifice form
A rep with bad form is not a rep — it's an injury. Drop the weight, nail the form, then progress.

Your Goals

Every element of this plan is designed around your specific targets.

💪
MUSCLE BUILD — Mon + Tue + Thu + Fri
Upper/Lower split twice per week hits each muscle group 2× per 7 days — the research-backed optimal frequency for hypertrophy. Monday & Thursday = Upper (chest, back, shoulders, arms). Tuesday & Friday = Lower (quads, hamstrings, calves, core). 2 sets per exercise to start builds the habit without overtraining.
🏃
CARDIO — Saturday + Daily Steps
Saturday run is your dedicated cardio day — choose Zone 2 (fat burning, easy pace) or intervals (VO2 max, hard pace). Additionally, 9–10K steps every day adds low-intensity cardio that burns 300–400 extra kcal daily without touching recovery. This is the most underrated fat loss tool.
🔥
FAT LOSS — Deficit + Steps + Lifts
Fat loss requires a calorie deficit. The 4 pillars of this plan: (1) 300–400 kcal daily deficit from diet, (2) 9–10K steps every day, (3) Saturday running, (4) 4× weekly strength training to preserve muscle while fat burns. All four together = fat comes off as fat, not muscle.
📊
RIR SYSTEM — Train Smarter
RIR (Reps In Reserve) means: how many more reps could you do with good form? RIR 2 = you stop 2 reps before failure. This is the optimal training zone — hard enough to stimulate growth, safe enough to recover between sessions. AMRAP on laterals = go to failure safely since it's an isolation movement with low injury risk.
