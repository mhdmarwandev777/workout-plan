Key Rules

🥩
Protein every meal
Spread 150g+ across 4–6 meals. Body uses ~40g max per sitting for muscle repair.
⚡
Carbs around workouts
Eat carbs before and after training — that's when they fuel performance, not fat storage.
💧
3.5–4 L water daily
Add 500ml extra on run and lift days. UAE heat increases sweat loss significantly.
📉
Rest day: ~2,200 kcal
Drop carbs by 50g on rest days only. Keep protein exactly the same.

Progressive Overload

10-week strength cycle. Never increase weight AND volume at the same time.

Week 1–2
3 × 10–12
Light — 60% max. Form & technique only.
Week 3–4
3 × 8–10
Add 5% weight. Base strength building begins.
Week 5–6
4 × 8–10
Add 5% weight + increase to 4 working sets.
Week 7–8
4 × 6–8
Heavier loads — add 5–10%. Push intensity.
Week 9
3 × 12 light
⚡ Deload — 50% max. Full recovery week.
Week 10+
Repeat
New baseline is +10% from start. Repeat from Week 3.

The 4 Rules

+2
The 2-rep rule
Can do 2+ extra reps with good form? Add weight. Upper body +2.5 kg, lower body +5 kg.
📝
Track everything
Log weight, sets, reps every session. You cannot progress what you don't measure.
⚡
Deload every 8–9 weeks
Drop to 50% intensity for one full week. Resets CNS and prevents cumulative injury.
🚫
Never sacrifice form
A rep with bad form is not a rep — it's an injury. Drop the weight, nail the form, then progress.
