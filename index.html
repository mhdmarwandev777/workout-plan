Why intervals raise VO2 max
Running at 88–95% max HR forces your heart to pump maximum stroke volume. Over weeks, your heart adapts — it pumps more blood per beat at ALL effort levels. This is VO2 max. Easy runs maintain it. Intervals raise it.

Key Rules

Protein every meal

Extra carbs on Wednesday (intervals)
Add 50g extra carbs on interval day — banana, rice cakes, dates. You need the glycogen for Zone 5 effort.

Add 750ml extra on interval and lift days. UAE heat + hard run = high sweat loss.

Rest day: 2,100 kcal
Drop carbs by 50g on Sunday only. Keep protein identical — muscle recovery still requires it.
🥗
Belly fat: deficit + intervals
You cannot spot-reduce belly fat. Interval runs + being in 300–400 kcal deficit is the fastest path. Both together are what removes it.

Progressive Overload

10-week strength cycle. Never increase weight AND volume at the same time.

Week 1–2
3 × 10–12
Light — 60% max. Form & technique. Start intervals at 3 × 1000m.
Week 3–4
3 × 8–10
Add 5% weight. Intervals → 4 × 1000m. Base strength begins.
Week 5–6
4 × 8–10
Add 5% weight + increase to 4 working sets. Full 5 × 1000m.
Week 7–8
4 × 6–8
Heavier loads — add 5–10%. Push intensity. Interval pace faster.
Week 9
3 × 12 light
⚡ Deload — 50% max. Intervals drop to 3 × 800m easy. Full recovery.
Week 10+
Repeat
New baseline is +10% from start. Repeat from Week 3 with higher weights.

The 4 Rules

+2
The 2-rep rule
Can complete 2+ extra reps with good form? Add weight next session. Upper +2.5 kg, lower +5 kg.
📝
Track everything
Log weight, sets, reps, and interval pace every session. You cannot progress what you don't measure.
⚡
Deload every 8–9 weeks
Drop to 50% intensity for one full week. Resets CNS and prevents injury. Not optional.
🚫
Never sacrifice form
A rep with bad form is not a rep — it's an injury. Drop the weight, nail the form, then progress.

Your 4 Goals

Every element of this plan is designed around these specific targets.

⚡
VO2 MAX — Wednesday Intervals
VO2 max = how much oxygen your body can use at max effort. The only way to raise it is to run at 88–95% heart rate. Wednesday's 5 × 1000m intervals do exactly this. Easy runs maintain aerobic base. Intervals raise the ceiling. Expect +10–15% VO2 max improvement in 8–12 weeks.
🦵
STRIDE LENGTH — Thursday Leg Day
Stride length = hip extension range × glute drive force × hamstring elasticity. The Hip Thrust trains glute drive. RDL trains hamstring reach and flexibility. Nordic Curl trains eccentric hamstring strength. Couch Stretch and 90/90 unlock hip flexor range. All 4 together = measurably longer stride within 4–6 weeks.
💪
MUSCLE BUILD — 3× Strength Days
Tuesday covers chest, back, shoulders, arms. Thursday covers legs completely. Friday covers full body compound movements with deadlift as the anchor. 3× strength + 155g protein daily = muscle building even in a caloric deficit. The muscle prevents the weight loss from being "soft".
🔥
BELLY FAT — Deficit + Intervals
Belly fat cannot be spot-reduced by ab exercises alone. You need: (1) a 300–400 kcal daily deficit, (2) Zone 2 runs for sustained fat oxidation, (3) interval runs to spike metabolism for 24 hours after, and (4) heavy compound lifts to preserve muscle while fat burns. Abs training shapes the muscle underneath — the fat layer reduces through calorie deficit + cardio.
