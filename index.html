Key Nutrition Rules

Follow these to maximise results from your training.

🥩
Protein every meal
Spread 150g+ across 4–6 meals. Your body can only use ~40g per sitting for muscle synthesis.
⚡
Carbs around workouts
Eat carbs before and after training. That's when your body uses them as fuel, not storage.
💧
3.5–4 L water daily
Add an extra 500ml on run and lift days. UAE heat increases sweat loss significantly.
📉
Rest day calories: ~2,200 kcal
Drop carbs by 50g on rest days. Keep protein identical — you still need it for recovery.

Progressive Overload

10-week strength cycle. Never increase weight AND volume at the same time.

Week 1–2
3 × 10–12
Light — 60% max. Form & technique focus. Learn the movements.
Week 3–4
3 × 8–10
Add 5% weight. Base strength building phase begins.
Week 5–6
4 × 8–10
Add another 5% weight. Volume increase — 4 working sets now.
Week 7–8
4 × 6–8
Heavier load — add 5–10%. Push intensity on compound lifts.
Week 9
3 × 12 light
⚡ Deload — drop to 50% max. Recovery week. Non-negotiable.
Week 10+
Repeat cycle
New baseline is +10% from where you started. Repeat from Week 3.

The 4 Rules

These rules prevent plateaus and injuries.

+2
The 2-rep rule
If you can do 2 or more extra reps with good form, add weight next session. Upper body: +2.5 kg. Lower body: +5 kg.
📝
Track everything
Log weight, sets, and reps every session. You cannot progress what you don't measure. Use your phone notes app if nothing else.
⚡
Deload every 8–9 weeks
Drop intensity to 50% for one full week. This resets your central nervous system and prevents cumulative fatigue and injury.
🚫
Never skip form for weight
A set with bad form is not a set — it's an injury waiting to happen. Drop the weight, nail the form, then progress.
